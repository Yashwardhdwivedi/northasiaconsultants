FAQs
Employer FAQs
Is it truly free? What's the catch?
There is no catch. We charge employers absolutely zero — no registration fees, no shortlisting fees, no placement fees. Our business model earns revenue from candidates upon successful placement. This fully aligns our incentives with yours: we only succeed when you make a great hire.
How quickly will I receive a shortlist?
For most roles, you'll receive a shortlist of pre-vetted, interview-ready candidates within 48 business hours of submitting your job brief. For highly specialized or niche roles, it may take slightly longer to ensure quality — we'll always communicate expected timelines upfront.
What does "pre-vetted" actually mean?
Every candidate on your shortlist has completed: an initial CV review, a phone or video screening call, a technical or skills assessment relevant to the role, and a reference check. We also verify qualifications and certifications. Only candidates who pass all stages are presented to you.
Do you handle visa and relocation for international candidates?
We provide comprehensive guidance on GCC employment visa requirements, documentation checklists, and typical relocation packages. While the formal visa application is the employer's responsibility, we advise throughout the process and ensure candidates are prepared with all required documents.
What if a placed candidate doesn't work out?
We maintain regular contact post-placement to ensure both employer and candidate satisfaction. If a candidate placed by us doesn't work out within the agreed probationary period, we will re-engage our search and provide replacement candidates at no additional charge.
Can you help with bulk or multiple hires?
Absolutely. We regularly handle multi-position briefs — whether it's staffing an entire department, a project team, or ongoing rolling hires. Please mention your volume requirement in the job brief and your dedicated account manager will tailor a plan accordingly.
