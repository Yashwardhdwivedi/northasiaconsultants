Who We Are
Your Trusted Partner in Global Recruitment

North Asia Consultants is a specialized recruitment firm dedicated to connecting Gulf-region employers — and increasingly global ones — with top-tier talent across industries.

We leverage deep market insight, a vast candidate network, and a transparent zero-cost model to streamline your hiring process. Our professionals' skills and cultural fit align precisely with your organization's needs.

What sets us apart is simple: we charge employers nothing. Our revenue comes entirely through candidate placement success — creating a partnership built on results, not fees.

Mission & Vision
Our Mission
To empower businesses in the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries — and globally — by supplying exceptional candidates through a transparent, efficient, and cost-effective recruitment model that puts results first and charges employers nothing.
Our Vision
To be the partner of choice for Gulf-region and global employers seeking high-caliber talent, recognized universally for our integrity, responsiveness, and unwavering commitment to client success — from the GCC to every corner of the world.

Our Journey
How We Got Here
Foundation
North Asia Consultants is Born
Founded with a clear vision: to create a recruitment firm that charges employers zero fees, making quality hiring accessible to all organizations regardless of budget. Our unique model was built from day one around candidate placement success.
Gulf Expansion
Establishing GCC Presence
We built deep expertise across all six Gulf Cooperation Council nations — UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain and Oman — developing in-depth knowledge of local labor regulations, market dynamics, and cultural nuances that set our placements apart.
Industry Depth
Specialist Sector Teams Built
We expanded from generalist recruitment to building dedicated specialist teams for Oil & Gas, Construction, IT, Hospitality, Healthcare, Finance, Retail and Logistics — ensuring every candidate is assessed by someone who truly understands their field.
Today & Beyond
Going Global
With a strong Gulf foundation, we are now actively expanding into Asia Pacific, Europe, and beyond — bringing the same zero-cost, high-quality recruitment model to employers worldwide. Our network is growing and so is our ambition.
